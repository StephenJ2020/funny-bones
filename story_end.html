Funny Bones Halloween Adventure Story

It’s you! Thank goodness for that - I thought I was going to be stuck up there forever!

I’m sure you have lots of questions… let me explain… the other night I was at home minding my own business when there was a knock on the door.

I wasn’t expecting anyone so I thought it must’ve been some trick or treaters being the 31st of October. I opened the door to see a woman dressed in the most wonderful witch costume!

A great big wide-brimmed witches hat, a long crooked nose, it must have been a prosthetic but it looked as real as anything, and a long black dress with the most amazing embroidery and black silk.

I admitted that I had completely forgotten to get out to the shops that afternoon so had nothing to offer her.

She looked at me and said in the most chilling high-pitched voice “Well, it will have to be trick in that case…”. All of a sudden I felt my knees go weak and saw bright lights in my eyes.

The next thing I knew I was here, I could hear the neighbours downstairs calling my name but as loud as I shouted they couldn’t hear a word I said. It seems I must have been enchanted in some way but you managed to break the spell!

Help me down these stairs my dear and I’ll put the kettle on…how have you been? How is your job in the city?
