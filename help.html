Aim

This is a card game in which all 6 cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

How To play

This game can be played by any number of players. Flip the cards over by using your mouse or finger tap on mobile devices to reveal the pictures and match the pairs. The game ends when the last pair has been matched up.

Aim

This game is based on the original Whac-A-Mole. The aim of the game is to click on as many ghosts as you can before they disapper, see if you can beat your highest score.

How To Play

On the screen click start, as the game begins you will see little ghosts appear from the floor in a random order. If you are on your desktop use your mouse to click on a ghost before it disappears, but if on a mobile device you may use your fingers.
