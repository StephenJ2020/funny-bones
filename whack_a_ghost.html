Well done, you found your way into the Living Room. It has a rustic charm but don't let that fool you, there's definitely something strange going on in here when nobody else is about!

How to Play:

This challenge is very straightforward, simple press the "Play" button once you are ready to begin. Spooky ghosts will suddenly appear out of thin air and it is your job to Whack as many of them as possible within 12 seconds.

Simply use your mouse or your finger (if your device is touch-screen) to click/tap on the ghosts as they appear. Your score will be displayed at the top of the page and you must Whack 5 or more ghosts in order to successful complete this challenge.
